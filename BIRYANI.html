BIRYANI RECIPE

Biryani is a celebration of aroma, color, and flavor, with perfectly cooked, fluffy grains of scented basmati rice and tender chunks of spiced lamb.

Ingredients:

  • ONIONS
  • tomato
  • chicken
  • spices

Steps

  1. Marinating the lamb in yogurt and salt adds flavor, increases tenderness, and reduces cooking time..
  2. Caramelized onions provide a deep flavor base and a flavorful garnish.
